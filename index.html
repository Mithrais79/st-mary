Who We Are

St. Mary Romanian Orthodox Church welcomes Christians and those who seek to connect to Christianity in the Timmins area. We aim to make contact with and encourage others to join us in our life-changing Christian journey. We are a friendly Christian community where we welcome others to join us in our worship and service to God. Our vision is to make an impact for God, here in Timmins, Ontario by helping people understand the enriching messages of eternal hope given to us by Jesus Christ through His words and deeds. Everyone is welcome, no matter your age, beliefs, or background. Come just as you are - we'd love to get to know you better.
